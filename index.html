我們的承諾

Our Commitment

每一杯手沖咖啡都是我們用心挑選、專業烘焙、精心沖煮的藝術品。我們相信，好的咖啡能帶給人們一段美好的旅程。

Every hand drip coffee is an artwork that we carefully select, professionally roast, and meticulously brew. We believe good coffee can bring people a wonderful journey.

輕旅咖啡專注於單品手沖咖啡的藝術，我們嚴選世界各地優質咖啡豆，以專業的烘焙技術和手沖工藝，呈現每一支咖啡豆獨特的風味與靈魂。

Jaunt Coffee focuses on the art of single-origin hand drip coffee. We carefully select high-quality coffee beans from around the world, using professional roasting techniques and hand drip craftsmanship to present the unique flavor and soul of each coffee bean.

完美地點

Perfect Location

位於臺中市西屯區福林路461號，交通便利，環境舒適，是您品味咖啡的理想場所。

Located at No. 461, Fulin Road, Xitun District, Taichung City, with convenient transportation and comfortable environment.

專業手沖

Professional Hand Drip

我們的咖啡師均經過專業訓練，精通各種手沖技巧，能完美呈現每一支咖啡豆的獨特風味。

Our baristas are professionally trained, proficient in various hand drip techniques.

嚴選豆源

Carefully Selected Beans

直接與世界各地的優質莊園合作，確保每一顆咖啡豆都符合我們的嚴格標準。

We work directly with quality estates around the world to ensure every coffee bean meets our strict standards.
